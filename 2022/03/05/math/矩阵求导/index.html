    矩阵求导的本质

    矩阵\(\pmb{A}\)对矩阵\(\pmb{B}\)求导，就是矩阵\(\pmb{A}\)中的每个元素分别对矩阵\(\pmb{B}\)中的每个元素求导。

    例如：

    \(\pmb{A}_{1\times1},\pmb{B}_{1\times1}\)，\(\pmb{A},\pmb{B}\)中只有一个元素\(a_{1,1},b_{1,1}\)，只有一个导数\(\frac{\partial a_{1,1}}{\partial b_{1,1}}\)。

    \(\pmb{A}_{m\times1},\pmb{B}_{1\times1}\)，\(\pmb{A}\)中的\(m\)个元素分别对只有一个元素的\(\pmb{B}\)中的\(b_{1,1}\)求导，产生\(m\)个导数。

    \(\pmb{A}_{m\times1},\pmb{B}_{p\times1}\)，\(\pmb{A}\)中的\(m\)个元素分别对\(\pmb{B}\)中的\(p\)个元素求导，产生\(m\times p\)个导数。

    \(\pmb{A}_{m\times n},\pmb{B}_{p\times q}\)，\(\pmb{A}\)中的\(m\times n\)个元素分别对\(\pmb{B}\)中的\(p\times q\)个元素求导，产生\(m\times n \times p\times q\)个导数。

    标量函数：\(y=f(\pmb{x}),y\in \mathbb{R}\)

    向量函数：\(\pmb{y}=f(\pmb{x}),\pmb{y}\in \mathbb{R}^d,d>1\)

    以分母布局为例的矩阵求导

    基本原则

    1. \(f(\pmb{X})=f(x_1,x_2,\dots,x_p)\)为标量函数，\(\pmb{X}=(x_1,x_2,\dots,x_p)^\text{T}\)为列向量，定义： \[ \frac{\partial f(\pmb{X})}{\partial \pmb{X}}=\begin{bmatrix}\frac{\partial f(\pmb{X})}{\partial x_1}\\\frac{\partial f(\pmb{X})}{\partial x_2}\\ \vdots\\ \frac{\partial f(\pmb{X})}{\partial x_p}\end{bmatrix} \] 即得到\(p\)个导数之后，最终结果的形式要和分母的形式统一。

    2. \(f(\pmb{X})=f(x_1,x_2,\dots,x_p)\)是向量函数，分母\(x\)是标量，定义： \[ \frac{\partial f(\pmb{X})}{\partial x}=\begin{bmatrix}\frac{\partial f(\pmb{X})}{\partial x_1},\frac{\partial f(\pmb{X})}{\partial x_2}, \dots ,\frac{\partial f(\pmb{X})}{\partial x_p}\end{bmatrix} \] 分子部分为向量，分母部分为标量，最后的结果要写成分子转置的形式。

    基本原则的拓展

    \(f(\pmb{X})=f(x_1,x_2,\dots,x_p)\)是向量函数，分母\(\pmb{X}\)是向量，则向量间的导数： \[ f(\pmb{X})=\begin{bmatrix}\frac{\partial f_1(\pmb{X})}{\partial x_1}\\\frac{\partial f_2(\pmb{X})}{\partial x_2}\\ \vdots\\ \frac{\partial f_m(\pmb{X})}{\partial x_p}\end{bmatrix} \]

    其中\(f_i(\pmb{X})\)均为标量函数。此时向量间的导数可表示为：

    \[ \frac{\partial f(\pmb{X})}{\partial \pmb{X}}=\begin{bmatrix}\frac{\partial f(\pmb{X})}{\partial x_1}\\\frac{\partial f(\pmb{X})}{\partial x_2}\\ \vdots\\ \frac{\partial f(\pmb{X})}{\partial x_p}\end{bmatrix}=\begin{bmatrix}\frac{\partial f_1(\pmb{X})}{\partial x_1} & \frac{\partial f_2(\pmb{X})}{\partial x_1} & \cdots & \frac{\partial f_m(\pmb{X})}{\partial x_1}\\ \frac{\partial f_1(\pmb{X})}{\partial x_2} & \frac{\partial f_2(\pmb{X})}{\partial x_2} & \cdots & \frac{\partial f_m(\pmb{X})}{\partial x_2}\\ \vdots & \vdots & \ddots & \vdots\\ \frac{\partial f_1(\pmb{X})}{\partial x_p} & \frac{\partial f_2(\pmb{X})}{\partial x_p} & \cdots & \frac{\partial f_m(\pmb{X})}{\partial x_p}\end{bmatrix} \]

    【举例】：

    1. 已知\(f(\pmb{x})=\pmb{A}^\text{T}\pmb{x}\)，\(\pmb{A}=(a_1,a_2,\dots,a_p)^\text{T},\pmb{x}=(x_1,x_2,\dots,x_p)^\text{T}\)，求\(\frac{\partial f(\pmb{x})}{\partial \pmb{x}}\)。

      【解答】 \[ \begin{align*} \frac{\partial f(\pmb{x})}{\partial \pmb{x}}&=(\frac{\partial f(\pmb{x})}{\partial x_1},\frac{\partial f(\pmb{x})}{\partial x_2},\cdots,\frac{\partial f(\pmb{x})}{\partial x_p})^\text{T}\\&=(\frac{a_1x_1+a_2x_2+\dots+a_px_p}{\partial x_1},\dots,\frac{a_1x_1+a_2x_2+\dots+a_px_p}{\partial x_p})^\text{T}\\&=(a_1,a_2,\dots,a_p)^\text{T}\\&=\pmb{A} \end{align*} \] 我们由此得到了一个重要的公式： \[ \frac{\partial\pmb{A}^\text{T}\pmb{x}}{\partial \pmb{x}}=\pmb{A}\tag{1.1} \]

    2. 已知\(f(\pmb{x})=\pmb{x}^\text{T}\pmb{A}\pmb{x}\)，\(\pmb{x}=(x_1,x_2,\dots,x_p)^\text{T}\)，\(\pmb{A}\)是一个\(p\times p\)的矩阵，求\(\frac{\partial f(\pmb{x})}{\partial \pmb{x}}\)。

      【解答】 \[ \begin{align*}f(\pmb{x})&=\stackrel{p}{\underset{i=1}\sum}\stackrel{p}{\underset{j=1}\sum}a_{ij}x_ix_j\\&=\stackrel{p}{\underset{j=1}\sum}a_{1j}x_1x_j +\stackrel{p}{\underset{j=1}\sum}a_{2j}x_2x_j+\dots+\stackrel{p}{\underset{j=1}\sum}a_{pj}x_px_j\\ \frac{\partial f(\pmb{x})}{\partial \pmb{x}}&=(\frac{\partial f(\pmb{x})}{\partial x_1},\frac{\partial f(\pmb{x})}{\partial x_2},\dots,\frac{\partial f(\pmb{x})}{\partial x_p})^\text{T}\\&=(\stackrel{p}{\underset{j=1}\sum}a_{1j}x_j+\stackrel{p}{\underset{i=1}\sum}a_{i1}x_i,\dots,\stackrel{p}{\underset{j=1}\sum}a_{pj}x_j+\stackrel{p}{\underset{i=1}\sum}a_{ip}x_i)^\text{T}\\&=(\stackrel{p}{\underset{j=1}\sum}a_{1j}x_j,\stackrel{p}{\underset{j=1}\sum}a_{2j}x_j,\dots,\stackrel{p}{\underset{j=1}\sum}a_{pj}x_j)^\text{T}+(\stackrel{p}{\underset{i=1}\sum}a_{i1}x_i,\stackrel{p}{\underset{i=1}\sum}a_{i2}x_i,\dots,\stackrel{p}{\underset{i=1}\sum}a_{ip}x_i)^\text{T}\\&=\begin{bmatrix}a_{11} & a_{12}&\dots&a_{1p} \\ a_{21} & a_{22}&\dots&a_{2p}\\\vdots & \vdots&\ddots&\vdots\\a_{p1} & a_{p2}&\dots&a_{pp}\end{bmatrix}\begin{bmatrix}x_1\\x_2\\\vdots\\x_p\end{bmatrix}+\begin{bmatrix}a_{11} & a_{12}&\dots&a_{1p} \\ a_{21} & a_{22}&\dots&a_{2p}\\\vdots & \vdots&\ddots&\vdots\\a_{p1} & a_{p2}&\dots&a_{pp}\end{bmatrix}^\text{T}\begin{bmatrix}x_1\\x_2\\\vdots\\x_p\end{bmatrix}\\&=(\pmb{A}+\pmb{A}^\text{T})\pmb{x} \end{align*} \] 我们又得到了一个重要的公式： \[ \frac{\partial\pmb{x}^\text{T}\pmb{A}\pmb{x}}{\partial \pmb{x}}=(\pmb{A}+\pmb{A}^\text{T})\pmb{x}\tag{1.2} \]

    向量矩阵求导的常用公式

    \[ \frac{\partial\pmb{A}\pmb{x}}{\partial \pmb{x}}=\pmb{A}^\text{T}\tag{1.3} \]

    \[ \frac{\partial \pmb{X}\pmb{Y}}{\partial \pmb{x}}=\frac{\partial \pmb{X}}{\partial \pmb{x}}\pmb{Y}+\frac{\partial \pmb{Y}}{\partial \pmb{x}}\pmb{X}\tag{1.4} \]

    \[ \frac{\partial(\pmb{X}+\pmb{Y})}{\partial \pmb{x}}=\frac{\partial \pmb{X}}{\partial \pmb{x}}+\frac{\partial \pmb{Y}}{\partial \pmb{x}}\tag{1.5} \]

    补充说明

    注意事项

    （引用自：https://www.bilibili.com/video/BV1UK4y1t7bv）

    1. 注意求导的布局，一般来讲分子布局和分母布局的结果相差一个转置。
    2. 布局没有优劣之分，适合的布局是最好的。要保持计算连贯性。
    3. 使用导数矩阵的维度来判断别人使用了什么布局。
